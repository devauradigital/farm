About Sweet Vegetable Farm

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Sweet Vegetable Farm began as a small family operation in the fertile lands of Nigeria. What started as a modest patch of pumpkin cultivation has grown into a specialized agricultural business focused solely on producing premium-quality pumpkin flute.

We take pride in our commitment to sustainable farming practices that not only yield the most flavorful and nutritious pumpkin flute but also protect our environment for future generations.

Today, we supply fresh, high-grade pumpkin flute to households, restaurants, and supermarkets across Nigeria, staying true to the quality and care that marked our humble beginnings.

As we continue to grow, Sweet Vegetable Farm remains dedicated to innovation in pumpkin farming. By embracing modern cultivation techniques, efficient irrigation, and organic methods, we ensure our produce stays fresh, healthy, and safe. Our mission is to nourish communities with the best pumpkin flute while supporting local farmers and promoting a sustainable agricultural future.

Organic Practices

We use traditional farming methods combined with modern organic techniques to ensure the highest quality produce.

Quality Focus

Every pumpkin flute is hand-selected at peak ripeness, ensuring you receive only the freshest and most nutritious produce.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our premium products.

Where is our farm located?
Our main farm is located in the fertile agricultural region of Ogun State, Nigeria, with additional operations in nearby states to ensure year-round availability of our produce.
How can I order our products?
You can order directly through our WhatsApp number (+234 812 345 6789), via phone call (+234 812 345 6788), or by visiting our farm in person. We're working on an online ordering system that will launch soon.
What makes Pumpkin Flute special?
Pumpkin flute is our signature vegetable, carefully developed over 8 years of selective breeding to achieve the perfect balance of natural sweetness and tender texture. With its rich, smooth flavor, it complements a wide range of Nigerian dishes, from soups and stews to traditional favorites. Grown using our specialized organic farming techniques, pumpkin flute delivers enhanced taste and high nutritional value—making it a wholesome and delicious choice for any meal.
Do we offer wholesale prices?
Yes, we offer significant discounts for wholesale buyers. For bulk orders above 50kg of any single product, you receive a 15% discount. Regular business clients may qualify for additional benefits based on order frequency and volume.
What payment methods do we accept?
We accept bank transfers, cash on delivery (within Lagos, Ogun, and Oyo states), and mobile money payments. We're currently working on integrating card payments for online orders coming soon.
How do we ensure product freshness during delivery?
Our produce is harvested to order whenever possible and packed in specially designed breathable containers that maintain optimal humidity. For longer distances, we use refrigerated transport to ensure your vegetables arrive as fresh as when they were picked.
